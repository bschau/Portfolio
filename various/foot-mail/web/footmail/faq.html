FootMail - Frequently Asked Questions

FootMail is a joke, right?
No - dead wrong! I use FootMail a lot when on the move. I connect using a Nokia 6230 Mobile Phone. When I know I have mail it is easy for me just to enter part of the senders email address in the filter box and the select Filter to find the mails ...
But why is it text only?
Well, being on the move means I need instant access to my Inbox. So instead of spending time (and money) on downloading some obscure graphics I decided to make FootMail entirely text only. This means fewer bytes to download.
When replying, the original text is lost?
Yes .. on some devices. I've seen this behaviour on a Nokia 6230. It only allows for around 700 characters in a textbox. So, a Nokia 6230 will refuse to show the text.
